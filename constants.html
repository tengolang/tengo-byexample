Tengo by Example: Constants

Tengo has no `const` keyword. Constant-like values are typically module-level variables. For data that must not be mutated, wrap it with `immutable()` to get a runtime-enforced read-only value.

 
fmt := import("fmt")

pi := 3.14159265358979
greeting := "Hello"

fmt.println(pi)
fmt.println(greeting)

limits := immutable({min: 0, max: 100})
fmt.println(limits["min"])
fmt.println(limits["max"])
