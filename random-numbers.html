Tengo by Example: Random Numbers

The `rand` module provides random integers, floats, and permutations. Use `rand.seed` to initialize the generator for reproducible sequences.

fmt := import("fmt")
rand := import("rand")

fmt.println(rand.intn(100))
fmt.println(rand.float())
fmt.println(rand.perm(6))

rand.seed(42)
fmt.println(rand.intn(1000))
fmt.println(rand.intn(1000))
